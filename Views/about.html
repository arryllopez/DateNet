Who Are We?

DateNet was created by a team of 4 aspiring Software Engineering students. Our team had a vision in mind, of creating a website that would allow university students similar to us, to find meaningful relationships. Whether you are looking for love, or a new business partner. Our team wanted to create a website that covers all of your needs.

Our Purpose

DateNet is designed for those who seek more than just a romantic connection. We bring together individuals looking for both love and business opportunities in a single platform. Whether you are searching for a partner in life, a co-founder for your next venture, or both, DateNet helps you find meaningful relationships that can thrive both personally and professionally.

Why Choose Us?

DateNet is the only platform where you can search for both romantic partners and business collaborators in one place.

Our sophisticated algorithm goes beyond traditional dating or business apps, considering both personal and professional compatibility to offer matches that meet your diverse needs.

DateNet profiles allow you to showcase both your personal interests and professional goals, making it easier to connect with people who align with multiple aspects of your life.
